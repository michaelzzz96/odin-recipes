Chocolate Cake

Description

A chocolate cake is a divine creation, a symphony of moist, cocoa-infused layers that melt in your mouth with each heavenly bite.

Whether topped with velvety ganache or a sprinkle of powdered sugar, its indulgent richness makes it the quintessential dessert, capable of turning any moment into a sweet celebration.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix well until the batter is smooth and well-combined.
  4. Gradually add the boiling water to the batter, mixing continuously. The batter will be thin, but that's normal.
  5. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cakes comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for about 10 minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Once the cakes are cooled, you can frost and decorate them as desired. Use chocolate frosting, ganache, or a dusting of powdered sugar for a delightful finishing touch.
  9. Slice, serve, and enjoy your homemade chocolate cake!
